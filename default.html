Speaker Recognition

a field of audio and speech processing that detects, distinguishes, represents, and verifies speakers in audio signals

by Charles Iliya Krempeaux

Speaker Recognition is the field of audio and speech processing that includes:

Speaker Diarization
Who spoke when?
Speaker Embedding
The speaker's more or less unique voice identifier.
Speaker Encoder
Turn this speech into a speaker embedding.
Speaker Identification
Who is speaking?
Speaker Verification
Is this person who they claim to be?
